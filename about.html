About DreamHomes Vanuatu

Our Company

DreamHomes Vanuatu has been the leading real estate agency in Vanuatu for over 15 years. Founded in 2008, we have helped hundreds of clients find their perfect properties across the beautiful islands of Vanuatu.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive real estate services including property sales, rentals, property management, and investment consulting. Our team has extensive knowledge of both residential and commercial markets throughout Vanuatu.

Our History

Starting as a small local agency in Port Vila, we've grown to become Vanuatu's most trusted real estate partner. Our success is built on integrity, local expertise, and personalized service for every client.
